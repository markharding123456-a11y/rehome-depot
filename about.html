About Rehome Depot

Good furniture, second time around — in Grand Forks, BC.

We give good furniture a second life. Whether you’re furnishing a first apartment, refreshing a room, or hunting for that one piece with character — we have something for every home and budget.

Our inventory is a mix of carefully selected used items and pieces consigned by local sellers. Everything is cleaned, inspected, and priced to move. We’re proud to be part of keeping good furniture out of the landfill and in homes where it’s loved.

Drop in any time during open hours — the inventory changes constantly, so even regulars find something new on every visit.

What we offer

  • Used furniture & decor Sofas, dining sets, dressers, lamps, mirrors, art, and more.
  • Consignment sales Have quality pieces to sell? We’ll list them in-store and online for you.
  • Estate & downsizing help Moving, downsizing, or settling an estate? We can assess and take whole lots.
  • Local pickup & delivery Available in the Boundary area — ask us for a quote.
